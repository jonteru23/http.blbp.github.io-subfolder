Request Now Online!


Welcome to the Online Building Permit System of Barangay Looc, Calamba City, Laguna,
where you may request a building permit online.







How to Request a
Building Permit Online?




1. Fill-out a copy of Barangay Looc Building Registration Form. Click here to download form.


2. After filling out the form, send it along with the other requirements to barangaylooc2@gmail.com Barangay Looc's Gmail account.

NOTE: The item must be handwritten while filling out the form. Kindly attach your two valid IDs and Home Owners' Residency at the bottom of the Barangay Building Permit Form. And save your documents as PDF files.


3. Wait for the admins' approval for your building permit (1-3 hours for your approval). The admin will send an email if you are approve or declined. If your request was declined, please double-check the attached requirements in your email to ensure it is clear, not blurred, hand-written, and signed. And then repeat the process of requesting a Building Permit.


4. Once your request is approved, you can go to the Looc Barangay Hall to pay the construction permit cost and collect your building permit.






REQUIREMENTS

Barangay Looc Building Permit Form


Two (2) Valid ID's


Home Owner's Residency
